SLASummit 2018

If you are interested in Social Entrepreneurship, Latin America, and making a change in a target community, sign up for SLASummit 2018!

If you are interested in Social Entrepreneurship, Latin America, and making a change in a target community, sign up for SLASummit 2018!

Social Leadership in Action's main event is a yearly social entrepreneurship conference and case competition designed to discuss fundamental social, political, and economic problems affecting Latin American communities, and implement socially and economically sustainable solutions. With amazing speakers, diverse students from all over the world, and four development projects under our belt, this year’s conference promises to be better than ever. We bring together the best and brightest to talk social entrepreneurship and sustainable development, but this weekend is about more than just capacity-building. We ask our participants, “Can you positively impact the lives of at least 30 people with $5,000?" The team with the winning community project is rewarded during the Summit and shortlisted to participate in our Summer Fellowship. This will take place in the Latin American community and all participants are invited to apply after the Summit.

Last year, SLASummit welcomed almost 100 participants from universities and colleges across North America. For its 2018 iteration, we are opening registration to 120 participants from an even wider range of universities around the world.

Secure your early bird ticket at a reduced price before we run out!

Registration fees include:

  • Four-day conference participation with SLASummit participant package
  • Speaker series, workshops, and mentorship for project development
  • Meals for all days
  • Optional accommodation with a student host for travellers to Montreal
  • Networking cocktail with speakers, judges and sponsors
  • Pub Crawl through Montreal
  • The chance to receive a $5,000 grant and sponsored flight to develop a project with a Latin American community
